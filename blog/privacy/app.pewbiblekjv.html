Privacy Policy for PewBibleKjv

Last Updated: 2025-11-25

PewBibleKjv (“the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal or non-personal data. Specifically, the App:

  • Does not collect device information
  • Does not collect usage data
  • Does not track user behavior
  • Does not request or access personal information
  • Does not use cookies or analytics services
  • Does not connect to external servers to gather data

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services or SDKs that collect or process user data.

Children's Privacy

Since the App does not collect any data, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 or users of any age.

Data Retention

Because no information is collected, no information is stored or retained.

Security

The App operates fully offline and does not transmit any information over the internet, helping ensure user privacy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App functionality changes in the future. Updates will be posted with a revised "Last Updated" date.
